Donate

Syndie's development is a volunteer effort, though much of the design and implementation is done by the entirely-donation-funded fulltime developer jrandom. Syndie is being built as part of I2P's development efforts, and your generous donations help provide jrandom's very modest cost of living, as well as development servers and hosting for Syndie and I2P (coming to approximately $100USD/month).

Even small contributions of $10 or $20 help offset these costs, and if you can help, please do so (note that the contribution is for Syndie in the memo field).
